Get Top-Quality Hardware Equipment at Hardware Shops in Nashik

Regardless of you building a new house or renovating an old house in a particular look or style, installing the right hardware is of utmost importance. Hence, hardware fittings and accessories are all important in order to achieve the desired interiors as well as meet the essential requirements in the house. The top-notch quality hardware equipment consists of home hardware, kitchen hardware, door hardware, window hardware, bathroom hardware, and so on that are available at various hardware shops in Nashik. Be it any hardware tools or accessories, hardware stores stock all of them along with brand options to make your purchase classy. Let us go through the various types of hardware tools and accessories offered by hardware stores in Nashik.

Window Hardware - Be it window hinges, window hooks, window handles, window friction stay, window curtain rod, or any other window accessories, fittings, or tools, you can find the best of products in Nashik hardware stores.

Door Hardware - A range of door hardware tools, fittings, and equipment like door closer, different type of handles and knobs for doors, cabinets, etc.; door knockers, latches, keyhole, door kit, magnet and catchers, tower bolt, etc.; door locks, night latches, multi locks, main door locks, etc.; are offered. Kitchen Hardware - Various products like platform racks, glass holder, bottle racks, plate stand or plate holder, hanging accessories, cup and saucer holder, cutlery stand, gas-cylinder trolley, various types of hangers, and a lot more are offered under kitchen hardware option.

Bathroom Hardware - In Nashik hardware shops offer different type of bathroom accessories like towel rail, rope hook, towel ring, soap dispenser, toothbrush holder etc.; rack and shelve options like towel racks, front shelves, front/corner wire shelves , etc.; overhead shower models; different type of hook rails; mirror cabinets; exhaust fans; different types of taps, faucets, floor drains, stop cocks and angles, and so on.

Other Wardrobe and Furniture Hardware - clothes hanging rod, jewelry hangers, cabinet handles, key holder, mail box, mirrors, shelf support and brackets, glass fittings, curtain hooks, wheels and legs, furniture fittings, safes, etc., are offered.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Are hardware shops in Nashik open during the pandemic?

Not all but many hardware shops are open on specific days, following guidelines mentioned by the government.

2. Will hardware stores near me offer installation services?

Most hardware stores have professionals technicians that offer installation services at additional installation charges. Please inquire about this in advance.

3. Do the equipment and supplies come with a warranty?

Some of the products come with a warranty, while others don’t. Please check the manufacturer's warranty at the time of purchase to be sure.

4. Do hardware shops in Nashik give a receipt for the purchase?

Of course, yes, they do provide receipt without fail. If you find that your product is defective, you can show the receipt at the store and get it exchanges, subject to store policy.

5. What is the approximate cost of hardware products?

The price will vary depending upon the hardware product you wish to purchase. However, the approximate starting price range is between Rs. 30 to Rs. 100 and above.
